CSE-2213: Discrete Mathematics

Comprehensive Study Guide & Notes

Course Syllabus Overview

Select a module below to access lecture notes, formulas, theorems, and practice problems.

🧠

1. Logic and Proofs

Propositional logic, predicates, quantifiers, rules of inference, and various proof techniques.
🖇️

2. Sets, Functions & Relations

Set theory operations, types of functions, equivalence relations, and partial orderings.
💻

3. Algorithms & Integers

Algorithmic complexity (Big-O), number theory basics, modular arithmetic, and cryptography.
🔁

4. Induction and Recursion

Mathematical induction, strong induction, structural induction, and recursive definitions.
🎲

5. Counting & Combinatorics

Pigeonhole principle, permutations, combinations, and binomial coefficients.
📊

6. Discrete Probability

Probability theory basics, Bayes' theorem, and expected value.
🕸️

7. Graph Theory

Undirected/directed graphs, connectivity, Euler/Hamilton paths, and shortest-path algorithms.
🌳

8. Trees

Tree terminology, spanning trees, binary search trees, and tree traversal.
⚡

9. Boolean Algebra

Boolean functions, logic gates, and minimization of circuits (K-Maps).
