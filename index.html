Midterm Syllabus

Weight: 30%
🧠

Propositional Logic

Intro to logic, logical operators, converse/inverse/contrapositive, and translating English to symbols.

Classes 1 - 3
⚖️

Logical Equivalences

Algebraic laws (De Morgan's, Distributive), tautologies, contradictions, and simplifying complex propositions.

Classes 2 - 3 (Advanced)
🔍

Predicates & Quantifiers

Predicate logic, universal/existential quantifiers, De Morgan's for quantifiers, and nested operations.

Classes 4 - 5
📝

Basic Proof Techniques

Standard forms of numbers, direct proofs, proof by contraposition, and proof by contradiction.

Classes 6 - 7
🖇️

Sets & Set Operations

Subsets, power sets, Cartesian products, and core operations like Union, Intersection, and Difference.

Classes 8 - 9

Counting & Combinatorics

Basics of counting, The Pigeonhole Principle, Permutations, and Combinations.

Classes 13 - 15

Recurrence Relations

Modeling with recurrence relations and solving linear recurrence relations.

Class 16
🕸️

Graph Theory

Graph models, types of graphs, representation, isomorphism, paths, and connectivity.

Classes 17 - 20
🌳

Trees & Applications

Tree properties, Binary Search Trees (BST), tree traversal algorithms, and applications.

Classes 21 - 23
